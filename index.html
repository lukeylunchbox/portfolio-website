About me

Hi there! I’m a developer from Melbourne with a profound passion for all things tech. Recently I’ve been coding in Javascript, Ruby on Rails, React and more!.

Before moving into programming I had a background in business development and corporate education.

I love urban environments and have spent three amazing years working in busy cities of China and south-east Asia.

I have a strong entrepreneurial spirit and live my life inspired by the words of Theodore Roosevelt; “Do what you can with what you have where you are.”
